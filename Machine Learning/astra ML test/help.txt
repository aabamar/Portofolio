id - customer's unique ID
age - customer's age
job - customer's current job
marital - customer's marital status
education - customer's education level
default - has credit in default?
balance - average yearly balance
housing - has housing loan?
loan - has personal loan?
contact - contact type
day_of_week - last contact day of the week
month - last contact month of the year
duration - last contact duration in second
campaign - number of contacts performed during this campaign for this client
pdays - number of days passed after last contact from previous campaign; -1 means client was not previously contacted
previous - number of contacts performed before this campaign for this client
poutcome - outcome of the previous marketing campaign
y - target variable, has the client subscribed a term deposit?
